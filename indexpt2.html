How you can SAVE.

Here's a short video with different strategies you can employ to help you save money. Every dollar saved counts toward a future purchase, whether it be a fun game, a nice shirt, or a good book. You can even create a savings account at a bank! This will allow your money to grow on its own.

Why is money important?

Money can be exchanged for goods or services. When you go to a restaurant and you want to eat something that someone else made, you give them money for that item. When you need to cut your hair, you give money to someone to cut your hair.

If you don’t have enough money to buy these things, you can’t have them. Let’s learn what to do to earn money!

Balance your spending.

Pretend you have $50 dollars. You could buy a new pair of shoes for $40 dollars or buy Legos for $40 dollars. How much money do you need to buy both items?

Answer: $80. But you only have $50 dollars. How do you get both?

The answer is by getting more money. Where do you get more money? You can get more money through many ways!
